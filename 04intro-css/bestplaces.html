What are the best places?

These are the top 5 places in the United States to enjoy and experience the sport of Golf.

Scottsdale, Arizona

Features:

  1. Desert Landscapes
  2. Mountain Ranges
  3. Restaurants and Nightlife
  4. Resorts and the Spa

Pebble Beach, California

Features:

  • Ocean Views
  • The Spanish Bay Inn
  • Golf History
  • Multiple Golf Courses

Kiawah Island Golf Resort, South Carolina

Features:

  1. Villas and Rental Homes
  2. Pete Dye's Ocean Course
  3. Stunning Scenery
  4. White Sand Beach

Jacksonville, Florida

What features does it have?
Spa, Clubhouses, Resorts
What other courses are there?
Slammer and Squire, King and Bear, Dye's Valley
What are the best hotels?
World Golf Village, Sawgrass Marriott

Las Vegas, Nevada

Features:

  1. Las Vegas Resort and Spa
  2. Bali Hai
  3. Paiute Resort
  4. The Westin Hotel
